Welcome to the Huntington Hills, Inc. website. This site is designed to help the residents of Huntington Hills access information about the area we call home and share community information with their neighbors.

On this site you will find meeting notices, official news such as the Huntington Hills Association Meeting Minutes and news and events from other neighborhood clubs and committees. You will also find a useful neighborhood classified section and a section connecting you to important sites such as the Town and the County. You will find official County Tax Maps and EPOD maps on our site.

This site also contains a wealth of information relevant to Huntington Hills such as:

  • Documents: Click here, and you will have access to Tax Maps, the EPOD Maps of the Town of Irondequoit, the By-Laws of Huntington Hills, Inc. and updated information about projects affecting our area, such as the Spring Valley Project.
  • Gallery: This is a site where each of you can share photographs of interest for members of Huntington Hills or anyone viewing our website. Scenic pictures of the area, pictures of area wildlife, your home, or anything else of interest are all welcome. Just forward them to our Webmaster for posting.

Some areas of our website are for members only and will be password protected. Information about access to these areas will be published in the mailing of the minutes.

This site is intended to provide important information about Huntington Hills, Inc. as well as to share items of interest with others in our community. Please use the Contact section to comment on the site, its contents and to add your own contributions to www.HuntingtonHillsInc.org.
