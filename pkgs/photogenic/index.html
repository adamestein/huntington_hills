Hi, it's Jack with yet another oswd template. Recently I over heard that oswd lacked good gallery templates, so went ahead and made one. Of course, "good" is a matter of opinnion, but I hope you all like it.

You might (or might not) notice that I've included a css description box that pops up when you hover over the thumbnails. Unfortunately this feature does not work in explorer due to its limitations in pseudo classes.

I've also made a search form (non-functional), you can remove it without damaging the layout. Just remember to erase the input boxes, not the entire form. The form element contains the right edge of the header, and removing it would cause the right edge to loose its "roundness".
